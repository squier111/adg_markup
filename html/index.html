Disconnect Report

This program generates a report of accounts based on the selections below and do not have a "Disconnect Exempt Through Date" prior to the disconnect date. The arrears amount is determined by the due date entered below. Penalties are accumulated separately and are included in the account balance. The program may optionally turn-off metered services and create a disconnect work order.
